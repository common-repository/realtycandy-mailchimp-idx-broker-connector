Follow up with your IDX Broker leads using MailChimp.

== Description ==

Real Estate is a business that requires great client followup.  You have a MailChimp account and you have an IDXBroker account and you want to bring your IDX leads to MailChimp.  This tool is a great way to do that right from WordPress.

You will be able to signup your IDX Broker clients automatically to get DRIP email campaigns, newsletters, and blog updates through MailChimp.

Use the settings page to select which MailChimp list you want your IDXBroker leads to go to, and you are off to the races.  Our server will make the proper connections for you.
